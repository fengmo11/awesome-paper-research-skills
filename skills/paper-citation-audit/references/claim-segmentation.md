# Claim Segmentation

Break manuscript prose into citable units.

## Claim Fields

- Phenomenon: what process or observation is discussed?
- Entity: which organism, material, model, population, dataset, or system?
- Relationship: association, mechanism, comparison, effect, prediction, or method?
- Context: condition, cohort, task, experiment, domain, or time period?
- Boundary: what is not covered?

## Claim Risk Types

| Risk | Signal |
| --- | --- |
| Causal overreach | causal verbs with observational evidence |
| Novelty overreach | first, unprecedented, never, unique |
| Scope overreach | universal language from narrow data |
| Method overreach | benchmark improvement treated as field solution |
| Citation mismatch | citation supports background but not the claim |
